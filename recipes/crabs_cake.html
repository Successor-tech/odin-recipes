Crabs Cake

Back to Home DescriptionCrabs Cake is a delicious seafood dish made from lump crab meat. It is crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lump crab meat
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter (for frying)
Steps
  1. In a bowl, combine crab meat, breadcrumbs, egg, mayonnaise, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix gently to avoid breaking up the crab meat too much.
  3. Form the mixture into small patties.
  4. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Fry the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce or salad.
