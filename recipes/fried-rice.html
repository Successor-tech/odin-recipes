Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a classic dish made with cooked rice stir-fried with vegetables, eggs, and a choice of protein. It’s quick, flavorful, and perfect for using leftover rice.

This recipe is versatile—you can adjust vegetables, spices, or add chicken, shrimp, or tofu to your preference.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Optional: cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the beaten eggs and scramble until cooked, then remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Add a bit more oil if needed and stir-fry the mixed vegetables for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps, and stir-fry with the vegetables for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and mix them evenly with the rice and vegetables.
  6. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper, stirring well to combine.
  7. If using protein (chicken, shrimp, tofu), add it now and stir-fry for another 2 minutes until heated through.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Fried Rice!
